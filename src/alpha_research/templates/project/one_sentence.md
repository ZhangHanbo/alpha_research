## What this sentence should NOT look like

- ❌ "We achieve state-of-the-art on benchmark X."
- ❌ "Our method outperforms all baselines on task Y."
- ❌ "We propose a new diffusion-policy variant for manipulation."
- ❌ "We improve accuracy on Z by 3.2 percentage points."

## What this sentence SHOULD look like

- ✓ "We show that equivariance in the *action* space, not just the
  observation space, is what enables few-shot transfer." (MAML-style
  structural claim)
- ✓ "We show that the optimization landscape of contact-implicit MPC
  is piecewise convex in the *normal* direction of the contact manifold,
  and that this structure is what makes warm-starting tractable."
  (structural insight that constrains solution class)
- ✓ "We show that tactile feedback carries the sub-millimeter alignment
  signal that the depth camera fundamentally cannot resolve, and that
  this gap is the load-bearing explanation for why vision-only methods
  fail at insertion." (mechanism claim tied to a formal barrier)
